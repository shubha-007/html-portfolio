About Me

Welcome to my webpage!

My academic background in Instrumentation Engineering has equipped me with a strong foundation in problem-solving and analytical thinking, skills that I believe are valuable assets in the IT landscape. As I embark on this new chapter, I am enthusiastic about leveraging my skills and knowledge to contribute meaningfully to the IT industry.
